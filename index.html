Welcome to Cherub Bagel™

This is home to the recipe of the official Cherub Bagel™, an Evanston delicacy only available on Hinman Dining Hall's secret menu. This is great for when you're in a rush and need to eat while running to seminar. It is not recommended to those who are opposed to creativity.


Ingredients

Poppy Seed Bagel

Strawberry Spread

Bacon

Egg

How to Make a Cherub Bagel™

First, head over to the bagel station and grab a poppy seed bagel. Slice it in half if needed and place it in the toaster.

(Four minutes for a hot bagel with a crispy texture. Three minutes for a tan bagel with a warm, soft crunch.)

While the bagel is warming up, head over to the hot foods section to grab some eggs and bacon.

When the bagel pops out, take one half and spread a small dollop of strawberry cream cheese on it.

Take both plates of ingredients and head over to a table to assemble your Cherubs Bagel™.
